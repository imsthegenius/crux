Privacy Policy

CRUX  ·  YM Management Ltd  ·  Last updated: March 2026

CRUX is a Stoic quotes lock screen widget app. Your privacy is straightforward: we collect nothing about you.

No Personal Data Collected

CRUX does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. No name, email, device identifier, or usage data is ever gathered.

Local Storage Only

All app preferences and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device.

Quote Content

CRUX fetches Stoic quote content from a read-only backend service. These requests are not associated with your identity and contain no personal data.

In-App Purchases

CRUX offers a one-time in-app purchase processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit. CRUX never sees or handles your payment information — all billing is managed by Apple.

No Account Required

CRUX requires no account, login, or sign-up of any kind.

No Analytics or Third-Party SDKs

CRUX contains no analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or any third-party libraries that collect data.

No Tracking

CRUX does not track you across apps or websites. There is no behavioral profiling, fingerprinting, or cross-device tracking of any kind.

Children's Privacy

CRUX does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
